Mobile dashboard client

Install on your phone or tablet, point it at your PC’s server IP, and run any TruckDeck or Funbit skin beside the wheel.

  • Enable “Install unknown apps” for your browser or file manager.
  • Download and open the APK.

TruckDeck NAV mod

TruckDeck_NAV.scs · ETS2

Mirror your web-based NAV into the truck cabin — transparent GPS and PC displays for supported SCS and community trucks.

  • Copy TruckDeck_NAV.scs to Documents\Euro Truck Simulator 2\mod.
  • Enable the mod in the Mod Manager (place above truck mods if needed).
  • Install cabin GPS/PC accessories on your truck.
  • Open TruckDeck NAV on your phone or browser while driving.
